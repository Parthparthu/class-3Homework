So what are computer accessories?

Every device you attach to your computer but they are not built in within your device.

You could be having basic accessories, For example: Laptop covers, Printers, Mouse & Keyboards, Scanners, Headsets, Cleaning kits as well as repair equipment, and list goes on.

More advance computer accessories will include:- An external Hard drive, Webcams, Bluethooth microphones, Equipment for gamers, External CD and DVD drives, and network accessories like Router, and other things.
